Speculoos (Biscoff) Ice Cream

Description

Creamy, caramelly, & cinnamony with crunchy Biscoff chunks

Ingredients

Yield: 1 qt

  • Egg yolks – about 6 (3/8 cup / 3 oz)
  • Sugar – 1/4 cup (1.8 oz)
  • Powdered milk – 1/4 cup (23 g)
  • Corn syrup – 1/4 cup (2.9 oz)
  • Kosher salt – 1/2 tsp
  • Baking soda – 1/8 tsp
  • Heavy cream – 1 1/2 cups (12 oz)
  • Whole milk – 1 cup (8 oz)
  • Vanilla extract – 1 tsp
  • Ice
  • Cold water
  • Biscoff cookies – 8 oz

Directions

  1. In a small saucepan, combine egg yolks, sugar, powdered milk, corn syrup, salt, & baking soda, stirring with a whisk.
  2. Add cream & milk and mix, stirring with a whisk.
  3. Heat the mixture over medium heat, whisking frequently, until it reaches 170°.
  4. Add vanilla extract and mix.
  5. Strain the mixture into an airtight container.
  6. In a large bowl, combine ice and cold water. Place the ice cream base in the ice-water bath. Chill the bowl (with the ice cream base and ice-water bath) in the refrigerator for at least 8 hours.
  7. While the base is cooling, crush 6 oz of the Biscoff cookies into crumbs in a food processor.
  8. Chop the remaining Biscoff cookies.
  9. Transfer the Biscoff crumbs & pieces to a medium bowl. Chill the bowl in the freezer.
  10. Churn the ice cream in an ice cream maker according to the machine's instructions.
  11. Transfer the ice cream into the chilled bowl with Biscoff crumbs & pieces. Thoroughly mix with a spatula.
  12. Transfer the ice cream into an airtight container. Chill in the freezer for at least 4 hours.
