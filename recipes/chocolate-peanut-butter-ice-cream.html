Chocolate Peanut Butter Ice Cream

Description

Rich dark chocolate and creamy peanut butter

Ingredients

Yield: 1 qt

  • Cocoa powder – 3/8 cup (30 g)
  • Sugar – 3/4 cup (5.3 oz)
  • Corn syrup – 3/8 cup (4.3 oz)
  • Heavy cream – 1 1/2 cups (12 oz)
  • Whole milk – 1 1/2 cups (12 oz)
  • Peanut butter, smooth ‐ 3/4 cup (6.8 oz)
  • Ice
  • Cold water

Directions

  1. In a small saucepan, combine cocoa powder, sugar, and corn syrup, stirring with a whisk.
  2. Add cream and milk to the saucepan, stirring with a whisk.
  3. Heat the mixture over medium heat, whisking frequently, until fully combined.
  4. Add peanut butter to the mixture and whisk until fully combined. Heat mixture if necessary.
  5. Strain the mixture into an airtight container.
  6. In a large bowl, combine ice and cold water. Place the ice cream base in the ice-water bath. Chill the bowl (with the ice cream base and ice-water bath) in the refrigerator for at least 8 hours.
  7. Churn the ice cream in an ice cream maker according to the machine's instructions.
  8. Transfer the ice cream into an airtight container. Chill in the freezer for at least 4 hours.
