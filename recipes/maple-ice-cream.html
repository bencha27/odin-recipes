Maple Ice Cream

Description

Rich and smooth, made with dark robust maple syrup

Ingredients

Yield: 1 qt

  • Egg yolks – about 6 (3/8 cup / 3 oz)
  • Maple syrup, dark robust – 3/4 cup (6 oz)
  • Powdered milk – 1/4 cup (23 g)
  • Kosher salt – 1 tsp
  • Heavy cream – 1 1/2 cups (12 oz)
  • Whole milk – 1 cup (8 oz)
  • Ice
  • Cold water

Directions

  1. In a small saucepan, combine egg yolks, maple syrup, powdered milk, and salt, stirring with a whisk.
  2. Add cream and milk to the saucepan, stirring with a whisk.
  3. Heat the mixture over medium heat, whisking frequently, until it reaches 170°.
  4. Strain the mixture into an airtight container.
  5. In a large bowl, combine ice and cold water. Place the ice cream base in the ice-water bath. Chill the bowl (with the ice cream base and ice-water bath) in the refrigerator for at least 8 hours.
  6. Churn the ice cream in an ice cream maker according to the machine's instructions.
  7. Transfer the ice cream into an airtight container. Chill in the freezer for at least 4 hours.
